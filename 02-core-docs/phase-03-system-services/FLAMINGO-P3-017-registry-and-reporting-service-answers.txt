Q: v1 purpose of Registry and Reporting Service?
A: Internal operational registry + investor/admin reporting layer.
   Registry is operational only — not legal books-and-records.

Q: If Flamingo and Securitize are not fully aligned, how is this shown?
A: Show operational balance and legal/TA status separately. Never collapse into one status.

Q: Investor-facing reporting surfaces?
A: Holdings dashboard, transaction history, distribution history, downloadable statement.

Q: Issuer/admin-facing reporting surfaces?
A: Cap table, subscription/allocation status, transfer activity, discrepancy queue, distribution status.

Q: Must reports distinguish operational state from legal holder-of-record state?
A: Yes. Where relevant, must be explicitly separated with layer labels.

Q: Investor statement delivery channels?
A: Portal + downloadable PDF/statement. Optional email notification (notification only, not full report).

Q: Is tax reporting in scope for v1?
A: No. Explicitly out of scope.

Q: Export formats for v1?
A: CSV and PDF. JSON/API export is post-v1.

Q: Disclaimer posture?
A: Prominent disclaimer on all reports: operational registry is informational;
   legal holder-of-record matters governed by transfer agent.

Q: How to handle reports during an unresolved discrepancy?
A: Generate report with discrepancy banner / flagged sections.
   Use canonical wording: "Reconciliation review in progress".
   Must not hide or omit discrepancy.

Q: Report retention posture?
A: Versioned snapshots with timestamps. Generated report history maintained.

Q: Is there a report taxonomy and disclaimer standard?
A: Reference separate report taxonomy + disclaimer standard document (not yet defined).

FLAMINGO-P3-012 — Reconciliation Engine
ANSWERS FILE — Source: PHASE 3 LOCKED ANSWERS SOURCE BLOCK — CROSS-CUTTING ADDENDUM

Q: Reconciliation model?
A: Event-driven checks + daily formal reconciliation (LD-049).

Q: Reconciliation sources?
A: Flamingo Operational Registry vs Securitize TA vs Base on-chain state.

Q: Discrepancy categories?
A: TA_LAG, DATA_MISMATCH, CHAIN_SYNC_FAILURE, COMPLIANCE_INCONSISTENCY,
   WALLET_INTEGRITY_ISSUE, LEGAL_RECORD_CONFLICT

Q: Severity levels?
A: Info, Warning, High, Critical

Q: Blocking discrepancy set?
A: Legal record conflict, sanctions-related inconsistency, wallet compromise,
   unresolved high-severity quantity mismatch, issuance/transfer state conflict.

Q: Blocking response?
A: Freeze dependent actions, create discrepancy case, notify responsible roles,
   require resolution workflow.

Q: Discrepancy ownership model?
A: Reconciliation/ops first. Compliance/legal engaged as needed.
   TA-side discrepancies require Securitize engagement.

Q: User-facing wording for CHAIN_EXECUTED?
A: "Processed onchain" or "Onchain processing complete"

Q: User-facing wording for TA_RECORDED?
A: "Transfer agent updated"

Q: User-facing wording for LEGALLY_COMPLETE?
A: "Recorded and complete"

Q: User-facing wording for REDEEMED?
A: "Redemption in progress" (during) -> "Redeemed" (complete)
   NOTE: REDEEMED is not the same as LEGALLY_COMPLETE (LD-042).

Q: User-facing wording for discrepancy under investigation?
A: "Reconciliation review in progress"

Q: When can "Recorded and complete" be displayed?
A: Only after LEGALLY_COMPLETE state confirmed via TA signal.
   Must not be displayed at CHAIN_EXECUTED or TA_ACKNOWLEDGED.

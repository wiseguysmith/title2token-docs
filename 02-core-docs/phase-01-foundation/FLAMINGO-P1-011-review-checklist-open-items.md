## Internal Decision

- [ ] **UI-P11-001** — Determine whether additional conditional review checks are needed specifically for Phase 2 product and control documents. The current conditional checks in Section 8 (8.1 through 8.7) are derived from Phase 1 foundation scope and cover the main subject areas. Phase 2 documents will introduce new subject matters — investor intake flows, permission model design, operator console controls, cap table boundary, admin transfer policy — that may require additional conditional check sub-sections not anticipated in this initial draft. This item should be evaluated when Phase 2 drafting begins and the first Phase 2 documents are reviewed.

- [ ] **UI-P11-003** — Determine whether the review checklist should be extended to cover companion documents (prompt files and answers files) in addition to main documents. Currently the checklist covers main documents only. Companion files are subject to the policy in P1-010 (Assumptions and Unknowns Policy) but are not formally reviewed using this checklist. If the project team determines that answers files and open-items files should also receive a formal review pass, the checklist's scope definition (Section 2) must be updated accordingly.

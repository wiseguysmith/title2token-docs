ASSUMPTIONS USED
---
- "Compliance / Review Operator" is treated as a distinct platform role from "Platform Administrator"
  because they perform different functions at different states (UNDER_REVIEW vs. general admin).
  Role permission specifics are conditionally locked (CLD-003).

- Broker-dealer is listed as out-of-scope for v1 but included by name to prevent confusion and
  document the boundary explicitly. Its possible presence in specific offerings is noted as an
  unresolved item requiring legal counsel input.

- Custodian is listed as out-of-scope for v1. No custodian integration is assumed.

- ATS is listed as out-of-scope for v1. No ATS integration is assumed.

- "Legal Counsel" is treated as a role boundary item (advisory only, external) even though it is
  not a Flamingo platform role, because the document must make clear Flamingo is not counsel.

- "External Vendor / Integration Partner" is included as a generic category. The transfer agent
  is the primary v1 external integration. No other specific vendor is named.

- The blockchain execution layer is treated as a technical execution component without autonomous
  authority — it acts on platform instruction only.

- "Orchestration is not authority" is the guiding principle for all Flamingo boundary framing.

---

SELECTED ROLE DEFINITIONS
---
- Flamingo: platform operator — no legal authority, full operational authority within the platform
- Issuer: deal-specific SPV / fund vehicle — issues the security, not Flamingo
- Investor: holder of security interest in SPV — limited to request submission
- Transfer Agent (Securitize): legal holder of record — books-and-records authority
- Compliance / Review Operator: manual review role — advances UNDER_REVIEW state, no legal authority
- Platform Administrator: operational admin — no legal authority
- Blockchain execution layer: technical tool — executes instructions, no legal discretion
- Legal Counsel: external advisory — not a platform role
- Broker-Dealer: out of scope v1
- ATS: out of scope v1
- Custodian: out of scope v1
- External Vendor: integration boundary only

ASSUMPTIONS USED
---
- Four truth layers are used: legal, operational, workflow, and blockchain/evidentiary.
  This taxonomy cleanly maps to the three authority planes established in P1-005 (legal,
  operational, technical) with workflow separated from operational for precision.

- "Workflow truth" is treated as a sub-category of operational truth — the current state of the
  platform state machine. It is distinct from the broader operational registry in that workflow
  state can change without a registry write, but in practice they are tightly coupled.

- "Flamingo reflects" is used to describe states like TA_RECORDED and LEGALLY_COMPLETE where
  Flamingo holds an operational representation of an external legal event. Flamingo does not own
  these records; it reflects confirmed signals from the TA.

- The "divergence window" between CHAIN_EXECUTED and TA_RECORDED is explicitly called out in
  Section 8 (Token Representation interpretation note) because this is the highest-risk period
  for source-of-truth confusion in tokenized securities platforms.

- Reconciliation status is treated as a Flamingo-owned operational artifact that reflects the
  result of comparing Flamingo records against TA signals. It does not constitute a legal
  determination in either direction.

- Investor security interest is the single most important source-of-truth boundary — the TA's
  legal record governs, not Flamingo's registry and not the token state. This is called out
  explicitly in the matrix with a "Critical" note.

OBJECTS AND STATES INCLUDED
---
Identity and entity records:
- Issuer identity, offering identity, investor identity / account reference, investor security
  interest

Token records:
- Token representation, transfer restriction status

Transfer lifecycle states (all 8):
- REQUESTED, UNDER_REVIEW, APPROVED, CHAIN_EXECUTED, TA_INSTRUCTION_SENT, TA_ACKNOWLEDGED,
  TA_RECORDED, LEGALLY_COMPLETE

Instruction and status records:
- TA instruction record, TA acknowledgment record, TA-recorded completion, legal completion status

Review, audit, and reconciliation records:
- Transfer review status, approval status, audit / event log, reconciliation status,
  exception / break record

SELECTED SOURCE-OF-TRUTH PRINCIPLES
---
STP-001: TA legal books are the authoritative SOT for investor ownership
STP-002: Flamingo operational registry is authoritative for workflow/operational tracking only
STP-003: Blockchain logs are evidentiary only — not legal books
STP-004: TA supersedes Flamingo in all conflicts
STP-005: Workflow state does not create legal finality
STP-006: LEGALLY_COMPLETE requires confirmed TA_RECORDED
STP-007: Operational and legal record ownership do not overlap
STP-008: Ambiguous status resolves in favor of explicitly defined authoritative system
STP-009: TA_ACKNOWLEDGED ≠ TA_RECORDED
STP-010: Token state is evidentiary; it does not constitute legal proof of ownership independent
          of the TA's records
